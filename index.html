CROP-leaderboard
A standardized benchmark for certified robustness of RL algorithms

TThe goal of CROP-leaderboard is to systematically certify the robustness of different RL algorithms based on certification criteria such as per-state action and the lower bound of cumulative reward. The related paper can be found here.
